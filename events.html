Imperial Gaming’s Beach Cleanup Drive:
A Step Towards a Cleaner Coastline

About the Initiative

Imperial Gaming is excited to announce its latest CSR initiative—a beach cleanup drive aimed at preserving Sri Lanka’s stunning coastal areas. This community-driven effort will take place at two iconic locations: Galle Face, Colombo, and Galle Fort Beach, Galle. The cleanup aims to remove litter and debris that harm marine ecosystems while raising awareness about the importance of protecting our environment.

Our Commitment to the Community

As a company passionate about gaming and fostering meaningful connections, Imperial Gaming believes in giving back to the community in impactful ways. Our team, along with volunteers, will work together to restore these beloved beaches to their pristine state, creating cleaner, healthier environments for both residents and visitors to enjoy.

How You Can Participate

Participants will be provided with all necessary tools and safety gear, and refreshments will be available to keep everyone energized throughout the day. We invite all eco-conscious individuals and organizations to join hands with us in making a difference.

Join Us Today

Together, we can protect these beautiful coastal landmarks for future generations. Let’s combine the spirit of teamwork that gaming teaches us with the goal of sustaining our planet.
