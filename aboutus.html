About Us

Welcome to Imperial Gaming

Your ultimate gaming destination in Sri Lanka! With four branches— Sentinel in Colombo, Valkyrie in Kandy, Titan in Anuradhapura, and Knight in Galle—Imperial Gaming brings top-tier gaming experiences to every corner of the island.

What We Offer

At Imperial Gaming, we cater to all your gaming needs. We stock:
  • Cutting-edge PCs and gaming hardware.
  • Popular consoles like PlayStation, Xbox, and Nintendo Switch.
  • A vast library of games for every platform.
Whether you’re a fan of high-octane action games or engaging RPGs, you’ll find something to suit your taste.

Expert Support and Events

We’re more than just a store. Imperial Gaming offers:
  • Technical support to help you set up and maintain equipment.
  • Gameplay assistance to enhance your gaming experience.
  • Exciting gaming tournaments and events to connect players.
Our events foster a vibrant community where gamers can compete, learn, and grow together.

Making a Difference

At Imperial Gaming, we believe in giving back. Our CSR projects, such as beach cleanups, unite gamers to contribute positively to the environment and society. Gaming is more than fun—it’s a way to inspire change.

Join the Imperial Gaming Community

Whether you’re a pro gamer, casual player, or a complete beginner, Imperial Gaming is here to power up your experience. Visit one of our branches today and explore a world where passion and technology meet. Together, let’s elevate gaming to the next level!

Sentinel is also a hub for exciting events, from tournaments to workshops, bringing gamers together. Conveniently located and packed with everything you need, Sentinel makes Imperial Gaming the top choice for gamers in Colombo.
