Frequently Asked Questions

Our FAQ section addresses the most common questions to help you navigate Imperial Gaming with ease. Find answers about purchasing consoles, games, and related services—all in one place.

What products does Imperial Gaming sell?

We offer a wide range of gaming consoles, video games, accessories. Check our product catalog for the latest additions to our store

Do you offer warranties on gaming consoles?

Yes, all gaming consoles come with a standard manufacturer’s warranty. Additional extended warranty options are available during the checkout process.

Do you sell digital game codes?

Yes, we provide digital game codes for select titles on platforms. These codes can be redeemed directly on their respective gaming systems.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, debit cards, PayPal, and other payment methods. You can view all available options during the checkout process

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes, we provide fast and secure delivery services nationwide to ensure your order arrives safely. Shipping options and costs are shown at checkout.

Can I trade in old consoles or games?

Yes, we offer trade-in services for select consoles and games you own. Visit our "Trade-In" section for eligibility details and estimated values.

Do you provide repair services for gaming consoles?

Yes, we offer professional repair services for gaming consoles of various brands. Contact us through the "Services" page for pricing and additional info.

Are there any discounts or promotions available?

We frequently run sales and special promotions on our store products. Check our "Deals" section or subscribe to our newsletter to stay updated.

Can I purchase gift cards for Imperial Gaming?

Yes, we offer gift cards that can be used to purchase items. They’re a great gift option for any gamer in your life

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team through the "Contact Us" page on our website
